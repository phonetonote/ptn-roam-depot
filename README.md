phonetonote helps you capture your thoughts on the go.

## ptn dashboard

access ptn configuration and advanced features at https://dashboard.phonetonote.com/

## how does it work?

after installing and configuring your account, you'll be able to

- send text messages to a private number that will sync to your roam graph
- send messages to the ptn telegram and facebook bots that will sync to your roam graph
- configure zapier integrations to post messages into roam

## usage

after installing the plugin, a link to your ptn dashboard will appear in your roam nav.

your phonetonote account will be created with the same email you tied to your roam account.

from your dashboard, you can configure your account, and add your phone number.

you can access the dashboard outside of roam by logging into https://dashboard.phonetonote.com/ with your roam email. we do not use any passwords. you will be sent a verification code to your email when logging in.

## fetching new notes

new notes will be pulled into your graph automatically when you open or refresh roam, and every 90 seconds after.

you can trigger a manual sync by _clicking the Daily Notes link_ in the roam nav.
